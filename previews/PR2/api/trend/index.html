Buff.Trend.detrend — Method
detrend(data; method=:linear, degree=1) -> Vector

Remove the trend from data.

Methods

  • :linear – subtract best-fit line
  • :polynomial – subtract best-fit polynomial of degree
  • :mean – subtract the mean
